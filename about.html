Our Story

St. David's Citadel of Learning, a private educational institution, was founded on September 13th, 2010. The school was born out of a passion to provide quality education, nurture young minds, and guide them towards fulfilling their true potential.

The first students to be admitted were our biological sons. We began operations in a rented ten-room building before moving to our permanent site behind Kajola market a year later.

Since inception, St. David's Citadel has stood firmly on moral, intellectual, and spiritual training. We are recognized for discipline, high moral standards, and academic excellence — with a strict zero tolerance for examination malpractices. Our focus remains on enhancing and bringing to life the innate abilities of our students.

Our Mission

To create knowledge, wisdom, and understanding that enhance total human development through innovative, life-transforming education that equips students to thrive in a competitive world.

Our Vision

To raise well-rounded students who are prepared for leadership and followership roles, having been equipped with the education and values necessary for success in life.
