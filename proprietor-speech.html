Meet Our Proprietor

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to St. David’s Citadel of Learning, Kajola, Ondo State a nurturing environment where young minds are inspired to excel academically, morally, and socially.

Our vision is to provide a balanced and holistic education that not only stimulates curiosity but also builds strong character and prepares students for a rapidly evolving world. At St. David’s, we recognize that every child possesses unique abilities waiting to be discovered and developed.

With the dedication of our passionate teachers, access to modern learning facilities, and a warm, supportive atmosphere, we help our learners explore their potential and grow into confident, responsible leaders. I warmly invite you to become part of our vibrant community of excellence together, let’s shape the leaders of tomorrow who will make a lasting impact on society.

Warm Regards,

Mr. OLAJOJO A.P
